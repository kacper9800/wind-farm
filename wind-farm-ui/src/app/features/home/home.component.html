Onshore Wind Farms

Onshore wind farms are located on land, where wind turbines harness natural wind energy to generate electricity. These farms contribute to renewable energy production, helping reduce carbon emissions and providing sustainable power for communities.

Offshore Wind Farms

Offshore wind farms are located in bodies of water, typically near coastlines. With higher and more consistent winds, these farms can generate significant amounts of clean energy, playing a key role in the transition to sustainable power sources.
